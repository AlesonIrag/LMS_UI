Privacy Policy

Protecting your personal information and academic data is our top priority at Benedicto College Library Management System.

Last Updated: January 2025

1 Information We Collect

Student Information:

  • Student ID number and academic credentials
  • Full name, email address, and contact information
  • Academic program and year level
  • Library usage history and borrowing records
  • Digital resource access logs and preferences

Technical Information:

  • Device information and browser type
  • IP address and location data (for security purposes)
  • System usage analytics and performance data
  • Login timestamps and session information

2 How We Use the Information We Collect

Academic Services:

  • Provide access to library resources and digital materials
  • Manage book reservations, renewals, and return notifications
  • Track academic research progress and resource utilization
  • Generate usage reports for academic planning

System Operations:

  • Maintain system security and prevent unauthorized access
  • Improve user experience and system performance
  • Send important notifications about library services
  • Comply with educational institution requirements

3 Your Privacy Obligations

Student Responsibilities:

  • Keep your login credentials secure and confidential
  • Report any unauthorized access to your account immediately
  • Use library resources only for legitimate academic purposes
  • Respect intellectual property rights and copyright laws
  • Update your contact information when necessary

Data Protection:

  • Do not share your account with other students
  • Log out properly when using shared computers
  • Report any privacy concerns to the library administration
  • Follow Benedicto College's acceptable use policies
